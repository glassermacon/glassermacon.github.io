Attorneys

Deeply rooted in the local community, Izaak Glasser and Clay Macon were both born and raised in the Tidewater area.

Izaak D. Glasser, a graduate of the University of Virginia (BA 1963, JD 1966) is known throughout the Tidewater area as an expert in real estate transactions. An approved attorney for all major Title insurance agencies, Mr. Glasser has conducted over 25,000 real estate transactions. He is a Commissioner in Chancery of the Chesapeake Circuit Court, and Escheator for the Commonwealth of Virginia.

Izaak D. Glasser

Clay L. Macon, a graduate of the University of Virginia (BS 1980, JD 1983) practices in the areas of real estate, divorce, estate litigation, civil litigation, personal injury, corporate formation, and contract litigation. Mr. Macon practices in the Circuit Court, General District Court, and Juvenile & Domestic Relations Courts throughout Southeastern Virginia.
